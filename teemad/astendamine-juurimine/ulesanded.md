# Astendamine ja juurimine — ülesanded

## 1. tase

**1.** Arvuta $3^4$.
<details><summary>Lahendus</summary>

$81$.
</details>

**2.** Arvuta $(-2)^5$.
<details><summary>Lahendus</summary>

$-32$.
</details>

**3.** Arvuta $\sqrt{144}$.
<details><summary>Lahendus</summary>

$12$.
</details>

**4.** Lihtsusta $a^3 \cdot a^5$.
<details><summary>Lahendus</summary>

$a^8$.
</details>

## 2. tase

**5.** Lihtsusta $\dfrac{x^7}{x^3}$.
<details><summary>Lahendus</summary>

$x^4$.
</details>

**6.** Lihtsusta $(2x^2)^3$.
<details><summary>Lahendus</summary>

$8x^6$.
</details>

**7.** Lihtsusta $\sqrt{50} + \sqrt{18}$.
<details><summary>Lahendus</summary>

$5\sqrt{2} + 3\sqrt{2} = 8\sqrt{2}$.
</details>

**8.** Arvuta $2^{-3}$.
<details><summary>Lahendus</summary>

$\dfrac{1}{8}$.
</details>

## 3. tase

**9.** Lihtsusta $\dfrac{(a^2 b^3)^2 \cdot a^3}{a^4 b^5}$.
<details><summary>Lahendus</summary>

$\dfrac{a^4 b^6 \cdot a^3}{a^4 b^5} = \dfrac{a^7 b^6}{a^4 b^5} = a^3 b$.
</details>

**10.** Arvuta $\sqrt{12} \cdot \sqrt{27}$.
<details><summary>Lahendus</summary>

$\sqrt{12 \cdot 27} = \sqrt{324} = 18$.
</details>

**11.** Vabasta murd juurest: $\dfrac{1}{\sqrt{3}}$.
<details><summary>Lahendus</summary>

$\dfrac{1}{\sqrt{3}} \cdot \dfrac{\sqrt{3}}{\sqrt{3}} = \dfrac{\sqrt{3}}{3}$.
</details>

**12.** Arvuta avaldise väärtus: $(\sqrt{5} + \sqrt{3})(\sqrt{5} - \sqrt{3})$.
<details><summary>Lahendus</summary>

$(\sqrt{5})^2 - (\sqrt{3})^2 = 5 - 3 = 2$.
</details>
