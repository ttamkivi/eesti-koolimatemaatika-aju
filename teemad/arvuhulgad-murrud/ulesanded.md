# Arvuhulgad ja murrud — ülesanded

## 1. tase

**1.** Taanda murd: $\dfrac{24}{36}$.
<details><summary>Lahendus</summary>

SÜT on $12$: $\dfrac{24:12}{36:12} = \dfrac{2}{3}$.
</details>

**2.** Arvuta $\dfrac{3}{4} + \dfrac{1}{6}$.
<details><summary>Lahendus</summary>

Ühine nimetaja $12$: $\dfrac{9}{12} + \dfrac{2}{12} = \dfrac{11}{12}$.
</details>

**3.** Arvuta $-12 + 7 - (-5)$.
<details><summary>Lahendus</summary>

$-12 + 7 + 5 = 0$.
</details>

**4.** Arvuta $\dfrac{2}{5} \cdot \dfrac{15}{8}$.
<details><summary>Lahendus</summary>

$\dfrac{2 \cdot 15}{5 \cdot 8} = \dfrac{30}{40} = \dfrac{3}{4}$.
</details>

## 2. tase

**5.** Arvuta $\dfrac{5}{8} - \dfrac{1}{6} + \dfrac{3}{4}$.
<details><summary>Lahendus</summary>

Ühine nimetaja $24$: $\dfrac{15}{24} - \dfrac{4}{24} + \dfrac{18}{24} = \dfrac{29}{24} = 1\dfrac{5}{24}$.
</details>

**6.** Arvuta $\left(\dfrac{2}{3} - \dfrac{1}{2}\right) : \dfrac{1}{6}$.
<details><summary>Lahendus</summary>

$\dfrac{4}{6} - \dfrac{3}{6} = \dfrac{1}{6}$. Siis $\dfrac{1}{6} : \dfrac{1}{6} = 1$.
</details>

**7.** Arvuta $-2\dfrac{1}{3} \cdot 1\dfrac{4}{5}$.
<details><summary>Lahendus</summary>

$-\dfrac{7}{3} \cdot \dfrac{9}{5} = -\dfrac{63}{15} = -\dfrac{21}{5} = -4\dfrac{1}{5}$.
</details>

**8.** Arvuta $3 - 2 \cdot (5 - 8) + (-4)^2 : 2$.
<details><summary>Lahendus</summary>

$3 - 2 \cdot (-3) + 16 : 2 = 3 + 6 + 8 = 17$.
</details>

## 3. tase

**9.** Teisenda $0{,}\overline{6}$ harilikuks murruks.
<details><summary>Lahendus</summary>

$x = 0{,}666\dots$, $10x = 6{,}666\dots$, $9x = 6$, $x = \dfrac{2}{3}$.
</details>

**10.** Kolm sõpra jagavad pitsat. Anna sööb $\dfrac{1}{4}$, Mart $\dfrac{1}{3}$. Kui palju jääb Kertule?
<details><summary>Lahendus</summary>

$1 - \dfrac{1}{4} - \dfrac{1}{3} = \dfrac{12}{12} - \dfrac{3}{12} - \dfrac{4}{12} = \dfrac{5}{12}$.
</details>

**11.** Näita, et $\sqrt{2}$ ei ole ratsionaalarv (tõestus vastuväitega).
<details><summary>Lahendus</summary>

Oletame, et $\sqrt{2} = \dfrac{a}{b}$, kus $\dfrac{a}{b}$ on taandatud. Siis $a^2 = 2b^2$, seega $a^2$ on paaris, järelikult $a$ on paaris: $a = 2k$. Asendades: $4k^2 = 2b^2$, $b^2 = 2k^2$, seega ka $b$ on paaris. Aga siis $\dfrac{a}{b}$ polnud taandatud — vastuolu. Seega $\sqrt{2}$ on irratsionaalne.
</details>

**12.** Arvuta $\dfrac{1}{1 \cdot 2} + \dfrac{1}{2 \cdot 3} + \dfrac{1}{3 \cdot 4} + \dfrac{1}{4 \cdot 5}$.
<details><summary>Lahendus</summary>

Kasuta $\dfrac{1}{n(n+1)} = \dfrac{1}{n} - \dfrac{1}{n+1}$:
$\left(1 - \dfrac{1}{2}\right) + \left(\dfrac{1}{2} - \dfrac{1}{3}\right) + \left(\dfrac{1}{3} - \dfrac{1}{4}\right) + \left(\dfrac{1}{4} - \dfrac{1}{5}\right) = 1 - \dfrac{1}{5} = \dfrac{4}{5}$.
</details>
